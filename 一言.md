> [!note] 胆小鬼连幸福都害怕，碰到棉花也会受伤，有时还被幸福所伤。
> Cowards are even afraid of happiness, they get hurt by touching cotton, and sometimes they are hurt by happiness.
> 出自《人间失格》作者：太宰治
